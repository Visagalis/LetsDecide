How It Works

From discussion to decision in three steps. No learning curve — just chat naturally.

1

Chat Naturally

Create a group, invite your team or friends, and discuss naturally with replies, reactions, voice notes, attachments, pins, and bookmarks.

2

Activate the AI

When things get complicated, tap the AI button. Choose overview, discussion analysis, or advice, include only the messages or attachments you want analyzed, and see the token estimate before running.

3

Decide Together

Get a structured summary, possible next steps, polls, and action items delivered right into the chat. People stay in control of final decisions.

Operational Overview

Missed 100 messages? The AI analyzes included messages and gives you a structured summary: key topics, decisions, open questions, action items, and files that influenced the outcome.

  • Topic-by-topic breakdown
  • Decisions & action items highlighted
  • Open questions flagged for follow-up
  • Consented attachments included in analysis

Structured Discussion Analysis

When a discussion gets stuck, the assistant compares stated positions, identifies stated agreement, and suggests questions or possible next steps.

  • Side-by-side comparison of positions
  • Consensus vs. dissent analysis
  • Resolution questions to move forward
  • Possible next steps with reasoning

Careful Suggestions

When tone matters, this mode offers careful wording and follow-up suggestions based on included messages. AI can be wrong, and people decide what to do.

  • Tone and wording review
  • Flags wording that may need clarification
  • Supportive suggestions
  • Focused on constructive follow-up

Frequently Asked Questions

Is LetsDecide really free?
Yes! During the Beta period, all features including AI analysis are completely free. No credit card required. We'll announce pricing well in advance before any changes.
Can the AI access all my messages?
You're in full control. Every message has an AI consent toggle — only messages you explicitly allow will be included in AI analysis. Same goes for file attachments.
Do I need to install anything?
No. LetsDecide works directly in your browser. You can optionally install it as a Progressive Web App on your phone for a native-like experience, but it's not required.
Can people join without creating an account?
Yes! Share an invite link and anyone can join as a guest with just a name. No signup, no email — zero friction. They can always create a full account later.
What languages are supported?
The app interface and AI analysis fully support English and Lithuanian. The AI will respond in the language your group uses.
How is this different from ChatGPT or other AI tools?
LetsDecide isn't a general AI chat — it's a real messaging platform where you talk to real people. The AI is a built-in tool that understands group dynamics: who said what, who disagrees, and possible follow-up questions. It does not replace human judgment.
What happens if a message is reported or deleted?
Reports preserve the reviewed message, nearby context, attachments, reporter details, and current message state so admins can investigate fairly even if the original message later changes.
