The real problem

Group decisions usually fail in the messy middle

Messages, evidence, preferences, owners, and final answers drift apart. LetsDecide keeps those pieces in one accountable conversation.

Without LetsDecide

  • Decisions disappear between replies, reactions, and side threads.
  • Files and voice notes are shared, then forgotten.
  • Nobody owns the next step.
  • Context gets scattered across chat, files, and tasks.

With LetsDecide

  • AI summarizes only the messages and files people include.
  • Polls, action items, and open questions stay attached to the thread.
  • Context stays tied to the final outcome.
  • Everyone can see the decision trail later.
Simple flow

From conversation to outcome

The product stays close to how groups already communicate. The structure appears when the conversation needs it.

1

Discuss naturally

Create a group, invite people, and use replies, reactions, attachments, voice notes, pins, and bookmarks.

2

Ask AI carefully

Choose overview, discussion analysis, or suggestions. Include only selected messages and consented attachments.

3

Close the loop

Turn the summary into a poll, action item, owner, or next question. People still make the final decision.
