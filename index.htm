Three Modes. One Smart AI.

Choose the right analysis for the moment. Each mode is purpose-built for a different kind of conversation.

Operational Overview

Missed 100 messages? The AI reads everything and gives you a clean, structured summary: key topics discussed, decisions made, open questions, and action items. Never scroll through a backlog again.

  • Topic-by-topic breakdown
  • Decisions & action items highlighted
  • Open questions flagged for follow-up
  • Attached documents included in analysis

Analytical Mediation

When a discussion becomes a dispute, the AI steps in as a neutral third party. It maps out each side's position, identifies where people agree, finds hidden common ground, and proposes fair compromises.

  • Side-by-side comparison of positions
  • Consensus vs. dissent analysis
  • Resolution questions to move forward
  • Compromise proposals with reasoning

Empathetic Advice

Sometimes you don't need a formal analysis — you need a wise friend. This mode reads emotional undertones, detects sarcasm and hidden feelings, and offers warm, relationship-oriented guidance to help people reconnect.

  • Emotional intelligence & tone analysis
  • Detects hidden frustration or sarcasm
  • Supportive, friend-like suggestions
  • Focused on repairing relationships

Frequently Asked Questions

Is LetsDecide really free?
Yes! During the Beta period, all features including AI analysis are completely free. No credit card required. We'll announce pricing well in advance before any changes.
Can the AI read all my messages?
You're in full control. Every message has an AI consent toggle — only messages you explicitly allow will be included in AI analysis. Same goes for file attachments.
Do I need to install anything?
No. LetsDecide works directly in your browser. You can optionally install it as a Progressive Web App on your phone for a native-like experience, but it's not required.
Can people join without creating an account?
Yes! Share an invite link and anyone can join as a guest with just a name. No signup, no email — zero friction. They can always create a full account later.
What languages are supported?
The app interface and AI analysis fully support English and Lithuanian. The AI will respond in the language your group uses.
How is this different from ChatGPT or other AI tools?
LetsDecide isn't a general AI chat — it's a real messaging platform where you talk to real people. The AI is a built-in tool that understands group dynamics: who said what, who disagrees, and how to find common ground. It's mediation-first, not conversation-with-a-bot.
